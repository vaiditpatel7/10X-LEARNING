“At 10X Studies, the vision is to make exam prep radically simple and accessible for every Class 10 student—clear pages, fast links, and trustworthy, NCERT‑aligned content that saves time and builds confidence. Built by students for students, this project keeps improving through real feedback, careful curation of PYQs, and a clean design that works on any device. The team believes small, consistent improvements—better structure, verified chapters, and reliable resources—compound into big results, helping every learner revise smarter and aim higher.”

We’re a four‑student team building 10X Studies to keep exam prep fast, clean, and reliable. Together, we plan the structure, write and verify chapter lists, code a lightweight interface that works on any device, and test every update for broken links and readability. This project is our way of helping classmates revise smarter, and we’ll keep improving it with your feedback and real exam needs.
